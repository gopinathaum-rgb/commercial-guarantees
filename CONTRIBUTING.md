# Contributing

Thank you for your interest in Commercial Guarantees.

## Ways to Contribute

- Suggest documentation improvements
- Share industry workflows
- Report inconsistencies
- Propose new guarantee products
- Improve examples

## Principles

Contributions should:

- Improve clarity
- Be evidence-based
- Avoid unnecessary complexity
- Preserve product neutrality

## Before Opening a Pull Request

Please describe:

- The problem
- Why the change helps
- Any supporting evidence or industry experience
