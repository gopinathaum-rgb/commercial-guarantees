# Frequently Asked Questions

## What is Commercial Guarantees?

Commercial Guarantees is an evidence-first architecture for documenting, monitoring, and verifying commercial guarantees throughout their lifecycle.

---

## Is this a financial guarantee product?

No.

This repository documents an operational model for commercial guarantees. It is not a bank, insurer, or regulated financial institution.

---

## Who is this for?

Examples include:

- Software development agencies
- Manufacturing companies
- Construction firms
- Engineering organizations
- Professional service providers

---

## What problem does it solve?

Many commercial guarantees end with issuing a document.

This project focuses on what happens next:

- collecting evidence,
- monitoring execution,
- recording claims,
- documenting settlements,
- enabling independent verification.

---

## Does it replace legal contracts?

No.

Commercial Guarantees complements existing contracts by organizing operational evidence throughout execution.

---

## Is this production software?

Not yet.

The public repository documents the architecture, concepts, and example products. Active implementation continues in a separate development repository.

---

## Can my organization adapt this model?

Yes.

The architecture is designed to be adaptable across industries with different guarantee workflows.
